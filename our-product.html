Challenges/Problem statement

Finops

With the advent of cloud native infrastructure setup, the organizations have been looking for the ways to:

  1. Get Consolidated financial reporting for Multi Cloud, Multi-Tenant, Per Service Breakdown and other customer models . Provide a view of Platform Administrator to Reseller Enterprise and to Tenant Administrators for their specific used services. Provide a platform for adding Margins and Discounts to different Tenants.

  2. Get Consolidated financial reporting for Multi Cloud, Multi-Tenant, Per Service Breakdown and other customer models . Provide a view of Platform Administrator to Reseller Enterprise and to Tenant Administrators for their specific used services. Provide a platform for adding Margins and Discounts to different Tenants.

Solution

IGCs, FinOps is a serverless technology to deliver the promise of multi cloud cost management by providing real time visibility and deep insights to help manage and optimize cloud spending in an agnostic manager.

AWS Checks:

Below are the checks as per AWS Recommendations that can be contributing to addition costs

  • Low Utilization Amazon EC2 Instances

  • Idle Load Balancers

  • Underutilized Amazon EBS Volumes

  • Unassociated Elastic IP Addresses

  • Amazon RDS Idle DB Instances

  • Amazon Route 53 Latency Resource Record Sets

  • Underutilized Amazon Redshift Clusters

  • Amazon EC2 Reserved Instance Lease Expiration

  • Amazon EC2 Reserved Instances Optimization
